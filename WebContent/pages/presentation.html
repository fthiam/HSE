Welcome to home sharing Expenses

This new application is amazing, you'll see!


Perfect when you organize group activities like trips, holidays, meals or when you share flat expenses with roommates! This application will make your life easier - let everybody add their own expenses into sub lists in particular groups and HSE will simply calculate the new balances of the group members.


Also, you can easely use this app to note tasks of everyone in your group, check if tasks are done... or not!
